Rus is a moon orbiting the planet Cyberia.

Cyc 59: Vladimir the Great of Rus invaded & captured the Byzantine city of Chersonesus on Ukrania. He offered to relinquish the city in exchange for the hand of Byzantine princess Anna Porphyrogenita in marriage. Emperor Basil II agreed, on the condition that Vladimir be baptised. Thus, the datascriptures of DEI_YESHUA were loaded into the servers of the court of Rus.

Cyc 62: Conquest of Rus by Omnigaol.

Cyc 64: The Standoff at Ugra. The armies of the city of Moskva defy the Golden Horde of Omnigaol, & cease all tribute thereafter.

late Cyc 64: regni Ivan the Great.

Cyc 65: regni Kaesar Ivan the Terrible.

Cyc 66: regni Kaesar Michael Romanov.

Cyc 67: regni Kaesar Peter the Great. Later, regni Imperatrix Yekaterina the Great. Completion of the conquest of Cyberia by Rus.

Cyc 68: regni Kaesar Alexander I. Siege of Rus by Kaesar Napoleon I of Gallia, with Rus victorious over the orbital invaders.
