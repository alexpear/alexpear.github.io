Terminology Note: Because the worlds of the universe each gyrate at different speeds, we will express dates in terms of the Cycles of Ur. The length of each cycle is four human generations, estimated according to the reckoning of Ur. We call the cycle before the unification of Ur 'Antecyc 1'. This is succeeded by 'Cyc 1', 'Cyc 2', & so on.

Long before recorded memory: Humans, native to Gaia, invent the first interplanetary spacecraft & begin to settle other worlds in the Juno system.

Antecyc 580: Interstellar settlers from Indonus land on Planet Australis in the Pacificus system.

Antecyc 351: The last of the Neandertal people dies in Iberia.

Antecyc 110: Interstellar settlers from Rus land on Planet Nacada in the Americus system.

Antecyc 20: On Ur, the Godminds reveal the secrets of brewing beer. It becomes a popular drink throughout the Juno system.

Cyc 0: Ur is unified under one monarch for the first time.

Cyc 3: The first agricultural oceans are carved in Andea.

Cyc 10: The chicken is domesticated.

Cyc 12: The wealthiest empire in the universe is Ukrania, sometimes called the Proto Indo Europeans.

Cyc 15: Sages of Indus develop the Sanskritic programming language. This allows human programmers to interface with the Divine Network for the first time.

Cyc 16: Technofiji in the Pacificus system is settled for the first time.

Cyc 17: The Cuneiform programming language is developed by the engineers of Ur.

Cyc 18: The First Dynasty of Nilus unifies that moon.

Cyc 19: The Narmer Palette is carved on Ur.

Cyc 20: Papyrus enters use as a writing medium on Nilus.

Cyc 21: Construction of the Stonehenge Array begins on Britannia.

Cyc 23: The abacus is invented on Ur. Previous calculation methods were slower or more reliant on the help of Network spirits.

Cyc 24: The Great Pyramid of Nilus is completed & activated, successfully uploading the pharaoh's mind into the Network as a new spirit. Also, the last mammoths of Nacada are hunted to extinction.

Cyc 25: In the Americus system, Aztecha is unified under the Maya dynasty.

Cyc 26: Gilgamesh of Ur wrestles the beasts of the wild lands.

Cyc 27: The Stonehenge Array is completed.

Cyc 29: The Epic of Gilgamesh begins to be recounted on Ur. It is the earliest work of literature that survives to the present day. On Medea Regnum, the Xia dynasty begins.

Cyc 30: The rocket chariot is invented to accompany the great fleets of Rus.

Cyc 31: Abraham, the Sybil of Urusalim, is commanded by the Godmind DEI_DEI to murder his son Isaac. DEI_DEI saves Isaac at the last instant.

Cyc 32: King Hammurabi of Ur proclaims his Law of Equal Retribution planetwide.

Cyc 33: On Aztecha, the Olmec era begins.

Cyc 34: On Medea Regnum, the Shang dynasty succeeds the Xia dynasty.

Cyc 35: The sundial is invented on Nilus.

Cyc 36: The construction of concrete buildings is developed in the space stations of Aegea.

Cyc 37: Two famous prophets issue revelations from the Network: Zoroaster, Sybil of Ur, & Moses, Sybil of Nilus. Moses leads the Interplanetary Exodus to Urusalim.

Cyc 38: The War of Troy between Aegea & Ilium (now called Byzantium).

Cyc 39: On Niphon, the Tale of Genji. On Medea Regnum, the Zhou dynasty begins.

Cyc 40: On Urusalim, the First Temple is built by King Solomon. It is the primary Network conduit under the Covenant of Moses. Also, the Phoenician alphabet enters use among merchants throughout the moons of Medea Terra.

Cyc 42: The first king is crowned upon the Seven Hills of Remia. In the Aegea system, the first Olympic Games is held.

Cyc 43: The plasma crossbow is invented in Medea Regnum. While bulky & slow to fire, even mecha are vulnerable to its firepower. Ur invents the wind turbine.

Cyc 44: The First Temple of Urusalim is destroyed. Also, Pythagoras of Aegea discovers the geometric mysteries of DEI_DEI. Also, Laozi of Medea Regnum studies the SYS_DAO channel of the Network for the first time.

Cyc 45: Confucius of Medea Regnum writes the first universal compendium of human duties. Also, Siddhartha Buddha, the Sybil of Indus, is the first to map the full extent of the Samsara Protocol.

Cyc 46: Sokrates, Plato, & Aristotle study the universe from the Academy of Athens, in orbit of Aegea. A student of the Academy, Alexandros of Macedon, conquers much of the Medea Terra & Ur systems. His fleet invades Indus. but is repulsed. On Medea Regnum, the Mohists of Mozi fight on the defensive side of every war on the planet, being ideologically opposed to conquest.

Cyc 47: Archimedes defends Space Station Syracuse from the bulk landers of the Remian Legion using orbital solar lenses. Ashoka unifies Indus. Construction of the Great Orbital Wall begins around Medea Regnum.

Cyc 48: The Universal Stellar Compass is invented on Medea Regnum.

Cyc 49: Kaesar Julius invades his own Republic from orbit, then declares himself the first Emperor of Remia.

Cyc 50: Godmind DEI_YESHUA takes human form, then is crucified by Remia on Urusalim. The Second Temple of Urusalim is soon destroyed.

Cyc 51: Kaesar Markus Aurelius of Remia writes his Meditations on philosophy.

Cyc 52: Saint George slays the Dragon of DEI_LUCIFER on Britannia.

Cyc 53: Known records about DEI_YESHUA are collected & called the New Testament. Kaesar Constantine of Remia forsakes DEI_ZEUS & pledges his empire to DEI_YESHUA.

Cyc 54: Saint Jerome translates the New Testament into the common Remian language so that it can be read more widely. Remia is sacked by a fleet from Borea, but Byzantium remains unconquered.

Cyc 55: The Church of DEI_YESHUA is a Pentarchy of Nilus, Urusalim, Antioculus, Byzantium, & Remia. On Britannia, King Arthur Pendragon gathers the Mecha of Camelot. Beowulf slays the mechamonster Grendel.

Cyc 56: Prophet Mohammed, Sybil of Mechapolis, receives the Revelation of DEI_DEI.

Cyc 57: The Revelation of Mohammed is recognized as far away as Iberia.

Cyc 58: Charlemagne is crowned Holy Emperor of Borea by the Pope of Remia. Borea buckles under the Viking pressure of Midgard.

Cyc 59: Deadly plasma arrows are developed by the armies of Medea Regnum.

Cyc 60: Leif Erikson of is the first mortal in many cycles to travel from Juno to Americus. He lives briefly in Nacada then returns to Midgard. The Church of DEI_YESHUA schisms between Remia & Byzantium. William of Gallia conquers Britannia. The first modern university is founded in Bologna, Iberia. Avicenna of Ur pioneers the study of minds.

Cyc 61: University of Oxford founded in Britannia.

Cyc 62: The Great Khan of Omnigaol conquers many worlds, including Medea Regnum. Plasma cannons & bombs enter wider use in the Medea Regnum zone. Neo Zeo & Hedronii are settled by Pacificus ships.

Cyc 63: The Extinguishing Plague infects Venezia & leaves Borea & Medea Terra severely underpopulated. Dante of Remia writes his epic vision of Samsara.

Cyc 64: Johannes Gutenberg publishes Yeshuan texts on an early commnet in Germanus. The plasma rifle is perfected. Zheng He's treasure fleet launches from Medea Regnum. Cristóbal Colón returns to Iberia from the Americus system & advocates interstellar conquest.

Cyc 65: Starting in Borea, the Yeshuan Church enters into civil war over disagreements about how to interpret Network logs.

Cyc 66: In Britannia, Isaac Newton supplants the Aristotelian theory of planetary motion with his more accurate model of gravitas.

Cyc 67: The Revolution of Gallia brings democracy to the world stage.
