- Game elements that should be visible
    - Round Wheel
        - It has 6 sections
            - The 5 non-current sections go in the 4 corners around the map, & 1 below the map
            - Current section
                - Current Step
                    - eg a Hive VIP
                        - or that we are midway thru a Eagle Card effect and will teleport back to another VIP after
                        - or that we are midway thru resolving a stack of card plays
                    - enlarged and highlighted
                    - Text saying who exactly needs to do what next
                - Other steps before/after the Current Step
                    - to the left & right of the CStep
        - Actions available to VIPs
        - Agents on VIPs
            - Agents have names
        - Supply steps
    - Map in center of Wheel
        - Places
        - Various things on Places
            - N troops
            - Agents on Places (advanced rule)
    - Scoreboard
        - display in South Pacific or other spare area
        - Current Hive (NPC) scores
        - Your current score 
            - (if game ended now)
    - Player areas
        - Indication of seating order
        - Your player area
            - Your loyalty circles
            - Your cards
            - Dropdown for which of your agent names you'll place next
        - Opponent player areas
            - Opponent loyalty circles 
                - often facedown
            - How many cards of each color they have 
        - 1st player marker (by a player area)
            - or just indicate this by them being at the top of the list 
    - Number of cubes off-board for each color
    - Number of cards in each deck
    - Log pane

- UX basics for this game
    - Top of the screen clearly says what is currently happening and who we are waiting on
    - Troops can only be placed on Places
        - and they snap into position
            - or perhaps are drawn as numbers
    - Place visuals
        - Troops do not obscure map symbols
        - Starting troop numerals not displayed at all
        - Hovering over
            - Under normal circumstances, it highlights the adjacent Places
            - When you are choosing a effect such as a Attack, it highlights only the permissible adjacent Places
        - Visually emphasize who controls a Place
            - but still show the default Hive, if it is a occupied Place
        - Harbingers should be very visually prominent
        - Emphasize when a Place is over supply limit 
    - It's easy to move N troops from a Place
        - perhaps via context menu
            - Pick up how many troops?
        - Or maybe you select start/end, then choose how many to move 
    - All your gamestate knowledge is always available without zooming in
        - such as
            - Everything that is at each Place
                - But you can zoom in if you want
            - What every Hive Leader does
                - but you can zoom in
            - What cards are in your hand
                - But you are likely to want to zoom in on each card - by hovering
            - How many cards everyone has
            - Everyone's loyalties 
                - mostly facedown ofc
            - The log pane
