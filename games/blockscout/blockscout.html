How to play Block Scout

This is an exercise-based mobile game about exploring your neighborhood. Play when you're walking, running, or biking. New squares are worth 1,000 points. Familiar squares start at 2 points & gain 1 point per day. Go to a square to claim its points. Have fun exploring!
