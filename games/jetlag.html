Jet Lag: Hide & Seek

California Version

The winner/s are whoever has the longest round as hiders.

Custom Rules:

  • Players may only move in these ways:
    1. On Foot
      • (escalators & elevators are fine)
    2. City Bus
    3. Trolley
  • The final hiding spot cannot:
    • Be off-limits to the public
    • Be somewhere that will close during the game
    • Be a bathroom
    • Be a store
    • Be a restaurant
    • Cost money to enter
    • Block players internet connectivity (eg basements)
    • Require climbing
    • Be dangerous
    • Inconvenience, disconcert, or creep out strangers
    • Be right next to a private car or residence (no looking like a thief)
    • Be in Mexico
  • This is a SMALL game, not Medium or Large.
  • Instead of 1 hider, a team of hiders may hide together.
    • If ANY hider is found, the round ends.
    • The hider team shares the hider role - shared hiding spot, shared hand of 6 cards, etc.
    • When the seekers enter the hiding zone, the hiders must be within 10 feet of each other.
  • Seekers must stay within 1/8 mile of their teammates at all times.
  • After 2 hours, any player may unilaterally end the round if they want (for strategy or for fatigue).
    • (2 hours counted from the end of the 30 minute hiding period.)
  • The penalty for accidentally breaking any rule is +/- 5 minutes to this round's final hiding time.
  • If you didnt break any rules but nevertheless have to change hiding spots for some unexpected reason (eg squirrels start attacking you), you may move to a calmer hiding spot nearby, & discuss it with the seekers after the round.

Reminders:

  • The default hand size is 6. If your hand becomes overfull, you must immediately play or discard cards before continuing.
  • At any time, anyone can ask to pause the game if necessary for health or comfort reasons.
  • 1/4 mile = 1320 feet ~= 2 North Park long blocks
  • If you're still on a vehicle when your 30 minute hiding period ends, and you choose a transit stop within 1/4 mile of you as your station, you haven't broken any rules.

In each round:

  1. The seekers must stay put for 30 minutes while the hiders travel to their hiding spot.
  2. After 30 minutes, the hiders must secretly choose a nearby transit stop (ie, a bus or trolley stop). This becomes their station for the rest of the round.
  3. Hiders may not be further than 1/4 mile radius from their station for the rest of the round. (They may move freely within this circle, however).
  4. When the seekers are also inside this radius, the hiders cannot walk & must stay wherever they are. (They don't have to notify the seekers, however).
  5. The round ends when a seeker gets within 5 feet of a hider.
  6. Then there's a 10 minute breather interval. Set up cards, dice, notebooks, & GPS sharing.
  7. A next round would start from this location.
