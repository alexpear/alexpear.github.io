Jet Lag

California Version

The winner/s are whoever has the longest round as hiders.

Custom Rules:

  • Players may only move in these ways:
    1. On Foot
      • (escalators & elevators are fine)
    2. City Bus
    3. Trolley
  • Hiding spots cannot be anywhere off-limits to the public, anywhere that requires climbing, in bathrooms, stores, restaurants, or anywhere that costs money to hide at.
  • Hiders may not inconvenience strangers or creep them out.
  • At any time, anyone can ask to pause the game if necessary for health or comfort reasons.
  • Players cannot leave SD County. (Is that even possible in 30 minutes?)
  • This is a SMALL game, not Medium or Large.
  • Instead of 1 hider, a team of hiders may hide together.
    • If ANY hider is found, the round ends.
    • The hider team shares the hider role - shared hiding spot, shared hand of 6 cards, etc.
    • When the seekers enter the hiding zone, the hiders must be within 10 feet of each other.
  • The penalty for accidentally breaking any rule is +/- 5 minutes to this round's final hiding time.

In each round:

  1. The seekers must stay put for 30 minutes while the hiders travel to their hiding spot.
  2. After 30 minutes, the hiders must be within 1/4 mile of a transit stop of their choice. (ie, a bus or trolley stop).
  3. Hiders may move freely within this hiding zone (1/4 mile radius).
  4. When the seekers are also inside the hiding zone, the hiders cannot walk & must stay wherever they are. (They don't have to notify the seekers, however).
  5. The round ends when a seeker gets within 5 feet of a hider.
  6. Then there's a 10 minute breather interval. Set up cards, notebooks, & GPS sharing.
  7. A next round would start from this location.

Distance Reminder:

  • 1/4 mile = 1320 feet ~= 2 North Park long blocks
