Villainverse of Neydis

Somewhere in the starry dance of Night, there is a world where Nature has tamed Humanity. Some wayward ancients ventured there long ago, but little knowledge can reach us from that place. The last letter received from such a visitor read, "Strange this new world is, but I have found my home. With these words I write my last."

Atlantis: The Illumineers of ancient Atlantis were familiar with the strange world of Avalonia - perhaps even with every world.
