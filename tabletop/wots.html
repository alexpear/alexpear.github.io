Whispers of the Stars

A board game of strategy & deception

Whispers of the Stars is a retheme for the game A War of Whispers by Starling Games, based on the final novel of Ada Palmer's Terra Ignota series. With this new theme, you play as one of the more subtle powers in the Universal Free Alliance, manipulating the balance of land control among the bigger Hives. It's pretty awesome.

This board game DOES contain some SPOILERS for book 4 of Terra Ignota.

To play this you will need to print this PDF, as well as gather 136 little cubes in 9 colors.
